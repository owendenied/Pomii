    Welcome to Pomi Timer

    How to Use the Timer

    • Modify your desired study duration and break duration in minutes.
    • Click the "Start" button to initiate the timer.
    • Stay focused during the study session.
    • Take a short break when the timer ends.
    • Repeat the process for subsequent study sessions.

    Managing Tasks

    • Type your task in the input field labeled "Add a task".
    • Click the "Add Task" button to include it in your to-do list.
    • View and remove completed tasks from the list.

    Enjoying Lofi Music

    • Access the LoFi Playlist
    • Listen to calming lofi music while using the Pomi Timer.

    Tracking Your Study Time

    • Observe the "Total Time" display to see how long you've been studying.
    • Stay motivated and focused throughout your study sessions.

    Customizing Appearance

    • Toggle between modes using the toggle button
    • Enjoy a comfortable viewing experience based on your preference.
